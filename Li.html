   Li Xuerui

Li Xuerui (China): Li Xuerui is a highly accomplished female badminton player who won the Olympic gold medal in women's singles at the 2012 London Olympics and several other major championships during her career.

Bibliography:

  • Li Xuerui's Achievements:
    Asian Chapmionships (2010, 2012, 2013, 2015, 2016)
    Asian Games (2014)
    World University Championships singles and doubles (2010)
    BWF World Tour (2018 - 2019)

  • Olympic Gold Medals: (2012)

  • World Championships: (2013 - 2014)
