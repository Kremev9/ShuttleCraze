Top Badminton Players of All Time

1. Lin Dan (China): Lin Dan, also known as "Super Dan," is often regarded as one of the greatest male badminton players in history. He has won two Olympic gold medals, five World Championships, and numerous other titles.

2. Li Xuerui (China): Li Xuerui is a highly accomplished female badminton player who won the Olympic gold medal in women's singles at the 2012 London Olympics and several other major championships during her career.

3. Taufik Hidayat (Indonesia): Taufik Hidayat is an Indonesian legend in men's singles badminton. He won the Olympic gold medal in 2004 and the World Championship in 2005, making him one of the sport's all-time greats.

4. Zhang Ning (China): Zhang Ning is one of the most successful female badminton players. She won the Olympic gold medal in women's singles in 2004 and 2008, in addition to multiple World Championships and All England titles.

5. Lee Chong Wei (Malaysia): is the best Malaysian badminton player of all time. No player in Malaysia has been that successful ever.
Lee was ranked 1st for 199 weeks in a row, from August from 21 August 2008 to 14 June 2012.

Here is the list of his trophies:

Five times Commonwealth champion;
Two times Asian championship winner;
One time Southeast Asia champion;
Three times silver medalist in the Olympic Games;
Three silver medals and one bronze medal in the World Championships.
